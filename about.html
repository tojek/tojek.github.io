About me

My name is Morgana, and for several years I've been involved in painting and makeup artistry. For me, art isn't just a profession; it's above all a passion and a way of expressing myself. Creating is my greatest love—every piece I work on isn't just a project but a personal story and an emotion I want to convey.

I'm most drawn to dark fantasy—I love creating worlds full of shadows, magic, and mystery. I'm inspired by mythology, fairy tales, and the darkness that often hides beneath the surface of reality. In my work, I combine classic techniques with my own imagination and sensibility.

On a daily basis, I work with clients who have a wide range of needs—I paint illustrations, portraits, conceptual designs, and I also do makeup artistry. I am open to diverse styles and themes, and I treat every project individually—with full commitment and attention to the smallest details. I believe that it's the details that build the atmosphere and make the works come to life.

~ Morgana

The Creative Process

I

Inspiration

Drawing from historical gothic imagery, folklore, and personal experiences with the uncanny.

II

Conception

Sketching concepts and exploring compositions that evoke both dread and fascination.

III

Creation

Layering textures and tones to build depth, working primarily in traditional media with digital enhancements.

IV

Reflection

Revisiting and refining to ensure each piece carries the intended emotional and atmospheric weight.
