About the Artist

I'm a painter drawn to the dark, the delicate, and the decaying. My work explores gothic themes—grief, transformation, myth, flesh—through layered textures and moody palettes.

With a background in SFX makeup for film and theater, I carry over an obsession with skin, scars, and subtle distortions. Off set, I paint, sketch, sculpt, and sew—building worlds where beauty and ruin intertwine.

Each piece is a quiet scene from some forgotten ritual or dream—a fragment of something sacred and strange.

~ Morgana

The Creative Process

I

Inspiration

Drawing from historical gothic imagery, folklore, and personal experiences with the uncanny.

II

Conception

Sketching concepts and exploring compositions that evoke both dread and fascination.

III

Creation

Layering textures and tones to build depth, working primarily in traditional media with digital enhancements.

IV

Reflection

Revisiting and refining to ensure each piece carries the intended emotional and atmospheric weight.
